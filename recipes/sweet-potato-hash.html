Sweet Potato Hash with Eggs and Bacon

Sweet Potato Hash with Eggs and Bacon Recipe

Sweet potato hash is a tasty and healthy breakfast dish that is made with diced sweet potatoes, onions, peppers, and seasonings. It's typically cooked on a skillet until the sweet potatoes are tender and crispy, and it can be served with a variety of toppings such as fried eggs, avocado, or salsa. Sweet potato hash is a great way to start your day with a nutritious and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 strips bacon, chopped into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and chopped into small cubes
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy, stirring occasionally. Remove the bacon with a slotted spoon and set it aside on a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
  2. Add the olive oil to the same skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped sweet potato, red bell pepper, onion, and garlic to the skillet and cook for about 10 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned.
  3. Add the smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.
  4. Add the cooked bacon back to the skillet and stir to combine.
  5. Crack the eggs into the skillet, spacing them evenly apart. Cover the skillet with a lid and cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the egg whites are set and the yolks are still slightly runny.
  6. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro and serve immediately.
